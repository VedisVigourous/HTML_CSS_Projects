Mr. Ferruccio Lamborghini

"WHEN YOU STOP WORKING, YOU START TO DIE"

Timeline of Ferruccio Lamborghini's Life

  • 1916 Born in Cento, Italy.
  • 1959 Expanded into manufacturing heating and air-conditioning systems.
  • 1948 Founded Lamborghini Trattori, producing tractors from surplus military equipment.
  • 1964 Launched the Lamborghini 350 GT, his first production car.
  • 1963 Established Automobili Lamborghini, aiming to rival Ferrari with luxury sports cars.
  • 1974 Retired from the automobile business, selling his stake in the company.
  • 1966 Introduced the iconic Lamborghini Miura, revolutionizing the sports car industry.
  • 1993 Passed away at age 76, leaving behind a dual legacy in tractors and supercars.
Ferruccio Lamborghini's response was to build cars that rivaled Ferrari and changed automotive history.
     "Lamborghini, you may be able to drive a tractor but you will never be able to handle a Ferrari properly." — Enzo Ferrari
